I was little upset with reading, attending meetups and even teaching people about design and other jargons (there are lot of fancy ones in UX), but…


none of them was helping me to in the way I think and communicate within my team at work.


I realized there is nothing right or wrong, everyone has just different way of solving a problem.


My way is to sketch with pencil asap and complete

the story on paper and then immediately move to a

digital tool (I already have a vision how it will work

and look in my mind, but I have to communicate my solution as clearly as possible to my product manager and to developers for a green signal).

Iterations and other adjacent ideas modify the base

solution or run parallel, but you are working with such a rapid speed that by the end of the day you always come up with one good workable solution that can also be deployed directly in production.

I hope this workshop helps you to be better than, what you already are good at.


- Anshul
